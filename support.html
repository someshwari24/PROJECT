Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Click on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation and fill in your details to create an account.
How do I submit my shopping details?
After logging in, you'll see a form where you can submit your address and shopping details.
How do I get matched with an assistant?
Once your details are submitted, the system will assign an assistant and open a WhatsApp link for you to connect.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We use secure encryption and do not share your data with third parties.

For more details on how we handle your information, please review our Terms and Conditions.
